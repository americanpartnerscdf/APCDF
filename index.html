Who We Are

Rooted in Community. Driven by Equity.

American Partners Community Development Foundation (APCDF) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that partners with local organizations, community leaders, and residents to create lasting, equitable change. We believe every American deserves access to safe housing, economic mobility, quality education, and a healthy environment — regardless of zip code.

Partner With Us

🤝 Community-Led

We follow the lead of the communities we serve. Residents shape our priorities and guide how resources are deployed.

📊 Accountable & Transparent

Our financials are publicly available. We pursue rigorous evaluation of every program to maximize impact per dollar.

🌱 Long-Term Investment

We focus on systemic change — not one-time fixes. We measure success over years, not news cycles.

🔗 Partnership Model

APCDF works alongside existing community organizations, amplifying their work rather than duplicating it.

What We Do

Our Programs

We operate across four interconnected focus areas, knowing that lasting change requires addressing the root causes of inequality — not just its symptoms.

🏘️

Affordable Housing Initiative

We support housing counseling, down-payment assistance, and advocacy for zoning reform to expand access to safe, stable homes for low-income families.

💼

Economic Opportunity Fund

Small-business grants, workforce development, and financial literacy programs that help community members build wealth and career pathways.

Learn More →
📚

Education & Youth Futures

After-school tutoring, college access coaching, and STEM enrichment programs for K-12 students in high-need school districts.

Learn More →
🌿

Community Resilience

Disaster preparedness, green infrastructure, and neighborhood improvement grants that strengthen communities against environmental and economic shocks.
